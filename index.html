   Idea of 99Grill was conceived in 2012 in (Amman, Jordan) with the determination of its founders to succeed. We were among the first restaurants specialized in crafting gourmet burgers and professional chicken wings, and we distinguished ourselves by preparing the majority of our ingredients (meat, chicken, sauces, etc.) manually. This posed a number of challenges due to the limited resources and the novelty of the concept. 

Within a year of launching the project, 99Grill emerged as one of the best restaurants in Jordan, becoming the preferred destination for Gourmet burger enthusiasts. With the growth of our customer base and increasing demand, we expanded into the local market and quickly began to spread across the Middle East and Africa (MENA Region) as one of the best agencies in the field of locally originated burger restaurants. Our current goal is to enter international markets, with our vision being the establishment of hundreds of branches worldwide.
